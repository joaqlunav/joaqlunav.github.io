Project Description

Background


LEXSYS is a legal first contact tool that's designed to provide clients with expertise on their legal problems whether they be a person or a company. Its main goal is to make legal consultancy accessible for everyone from a trusted and well prepared source. It is currently offered via phone, email and WhatsApp making it very slow, ineffective, and really hard to match up clients' issues with the correct firms that can solve them.

Goal & Proposed Solution


● Design & develop a MVP mobile application that improves the current service model.
